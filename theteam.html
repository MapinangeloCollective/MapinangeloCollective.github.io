About The Team

Jade Lacsamana

Jade has an Hons BA in Geographic Analysis from Ryerson University and an Ecosystem Mangement Technician diploma from Fleming College. She is currently completing the GIS Applications Specialist program at Fleming College. She most recently completed a volunteer internship as a Conservation Technician with the Nature Conservancy of Canada, which after contributing to Blanding's Turtle and ELC surveys, spurred the creation of this Collaborative Project. She is passionate about nature and hopes her work in GIS will contribute to environmental conservation.

James Kwasnicki

James has a degree in Physical Geography. He is currently working towards a graduate ceritifcate in GIS at Fleming College. He has a passion for the outdoors and has most recently worked in Banff National Park at the local ski hill.

Kerstyne Widmeyer

Kerstyne has a bachelors degree in Zoology/ Ecology and an Environmental Technician diploma. She's currently working towards a graduate ceritifcate in GIS at Fleming College. Most recently shes worked with the Trout Unlimited Canada on their Bring Back the Brookies campaign, bringing awareness to the importance of Brook Trout and the health of Twelve Mile Creek.
