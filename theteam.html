About The Team

Jade Lacsamana

Jade has an Hons BA in Geographic Analysis from Ryerson University and an Ecosystem Mangement Technician diploma from Fleming College. She is currently completing the GIS Applications Specialist program at Fleming College. After completing a volunteer internship as a Conservation Technician with NCC last summer, her experience contributing to Blanding's Turtle and ELC surveys spurred the creation of this Collaborative Project. She is passionate about nature and hopes her work in GIS will contribute to environmental conservation.

James Kwasnicki

James has a BSc in Physical Geography from the University of Regina. He is currently working towards a graduate ceritifcate in GIS Applications at Fleming College. He has a passion for the outdoors and has most recently worked in Banff National Park at the local ski hill.

Kerstyne Widmeyer

Kerstyne has a bachelors degree in Zoology/ Ecology from the University of Guelph and an Environmental Technician diploma from Niagara College. She's currently working towards a graduate ceritifcate in GIS at Fleming College. She worked with Trout Unlimited Canada on their Bring Back the Brookies campaign, bringing awareness to the importance of Brook Trout and the health of Twelve Mile Creek. This summer she's working with Entomogen Inc, an environmental consulting company, to ID and test mosquitos from all around Ontario for viruses such as West Nile and Zika.
